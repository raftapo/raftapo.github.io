I am an Assistant Professor (Lecturer) in Finance at the [Department of Banking & Finance](https://www.kcl.ac.uk/business/research/departments/banking-finance), King's Business School, King's College London. 

From September 2025, I am Programme Director for Data Analytics for Business & Finance BSc at King's Business School. My current research focuses on Machine Learning and Bayesian time series econometrics and their applications in macroeconomics and finance. 

Feel free to navigate on my website where you can find more details about my Research, Teaching and CV.
