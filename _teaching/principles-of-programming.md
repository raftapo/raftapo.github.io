**BSc · Instructor**

This module introduces students to the fundamental principles of programming through Python, one of the most popular and versatile 
programming languages. Designed for beginners, the course covers key concepts such as variables, data types, control structures 
(loops and conditions), functions, and error handling. Further, students will be introduced to basic data analysis, including 
descriptive statistics and linear regression, using Python. Students will also learn to work with Python’s powerful libraries and
tools, enabling them to solve real-world problems efficiently.

The teaching approach is hands-on, with a focus on practical programming exercises and projects. Through a combination of lectures, 
tutorials, and lab sessions, students will gain a solid foundation in writing, testing, and debugging Python code. They will also 
explore best practices for writing clean, readable, and efficient code, as well as techniques for breaking down complex problems 
into manageable parts.

This module equips students with both the theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed to pursue further studies in programming,
data science, software development, or other technical fields.
